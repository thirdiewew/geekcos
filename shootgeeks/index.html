    About Us

    Our Story

    Shootgeeks photography and videography is a team that renders services for commercial and non-commercial use. Businesses, schools and any establishments need photographs and/or videos to promote and advertise their products and for whatever purpose they intend, and so as families need photographers to capture the special moments of their lives. Families of professionals are busy, and if too much time goes by between photos, these moments are lost forever. Weddings, Christenings, Dedications, and Birthdays are only a few of those life events that we may treasure and keep by having it captured.
